# iMaxx (formerly iMaxx) Mascot & Gamification Vision

## 1. The Strategy: Mascot as the Ultimate Accountability Loop
Most ADHD and productivity apps fail because they feel clinical—like filling out a spreadsheet. A mascot (inspired by Finch's pet bird, GitHub's Octocat, and Duolingo's Owl) changes the user's relationship with the app from **dry self-discipline** to **emotional care**. 

By introducing **Corti, the Brain-Cloud**, we transform iMaxx/iMaxx from a utility app into a supportive companion. Users aren't just logging habits; they are helping Corti grow, focus, and feel energized.

---

## 2. Introducing "Corti": The Brain-Cloud Mascot
Corti is a cute, sentient, glowing neon brain-cloud character that acts as the literal embodiment of the user's cognitive state. Corti shifts shapes, glowing colors, and items based on the app's modes:

![Corti Emotions Sheet](file:///Users/umair/dev/mobile-apps/iMaxx/new-designs/corti_emotions_sheet.png)

1. **Focused (Orange)**: Wearing thick reading glasses, tapping on a laptop or writing in a notepad, glowing in warm focus-inducing orange.
2. **Sleepy / Calm (Purple)**: Floating on a soft cloud bed, wearing a nightcap, drifting in calm pastel violet.
3. **Brain Fog / Distracted (Grey)**: Tangled like a ball of yarn, looking dazed with tiny colorful question marks orbiting it.
4. **Celebrating (Green)**: Cheering, doing a happy dance, and launching colorful neon confetti when goals are checked off.

---

## 3. Core Gamified Features & UI Integrations

### A. Mascot-Guided Focus Session
Instead of a simple numeric timer countdown, Corti resides inside the progress dial itself. During your session, Corti focuses along with you. If you get distracted or close the app prematurely, Corti gets slightly worried. When you finish, Corti cheers, rewarding you with "Dopamine Sparks".

![Focus Timer Mascot mockup](file:///Users/umair/dev/mobile-apps/iMaxx/new-designs/focus_timer_mascot.png)

### B. The Habit Garden
Instead of just grey grids, your habits form a **Digital Garden**.
- Every habit you configure is represented by a small seedling.
- Completing a habit triggers a delightful micro-animation where **Corti flies down with a tiny watering can to water the tile**, causing the sprout to bloom into a beautiful flower.
- Higher streaks unlock exotic plant varieties, giving users a highly visible, satisfying sense of progress.

![Habits Garden Mascot mockup](file:///Users/umair/dev/mobile-apps/iMaxx/new-designs/habit_garden_mascot.png)

## 4. Viral Growth Loops & Mascot Customization

- **Relatable ADHD Animations**: Use Corti in short-form UGC videos (TikTok/Reels) to act out funny, highly relatable ADHD scenarios (e.g. starting a focus timer and immediately getting distracted by a shiny button).
- **Dopamine Points Shop**: Earn points by checking off habits and completing timer sessions. Spend points to buy cute accessories for Corti (e.g. tiny DJ headphones, a cozy scarf, or a golden crown).
- **Interactive Widgets**: Render Corti directly on the user's Lockscreen and Homescreen widgets, showing Corti's current state (e.g. asleep at night, working hard during calendar focus blocks).
