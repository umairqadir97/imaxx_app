🏠 Home — Companion Core
The user's home centers on Corti's room. Micro-interactions keep the user returning.

🧸 Physical Nurture Loop

Users can Pet or Feed Corti, instantly changing mood/energy stats and updating dialog lines. This bridges the clinical productivity gap with emotional care.

🧭 Adventure Quests & Exploration
Sending Corti on Quests models the central retention engine of Finch. Focusing or checks-offs fuel Corti's journey. When the quest finishes, Corti returns with Sparks and a journal entry detail.
🌙 Sleep — Wake Challenges
A Cozy wind-down room combined with robust Sleepy-style challenges.

📵 Wind-down checklist

Checking wind-down boxes makes Corti drop into a purple sleepy state with a nightcap. Sticking to bed routines increases calm multipliers.

🧠 Alarm challenges (Sleepy Moat)
Test the alarm simulation! Solve math riddle series or shake to dismiss. It ensures deep sleep sleepers cannot easily dismiss alarms and sleep back in.
🧠 SlowMaxx — Relax Soundscapes
Generative background acoustics & visual pacing tools to regulate brain waves.

🫧 Generative synthesizers

Unlocks Calm/Endel audience. Drag sliders for Forest Rain, Waves, Lofi Pad, and detuned Binaural audio. A real synthesizer generates relaxing beats directly in the browser.

🌊 Pulse waves visualizer
A canvas visualizer pulses and graphs frequencies dynamically. Tapping Corti starts breathing visual guide pacing (4-4-4-4).
⏱️ Focus — Co-working companion
Pomodoro with visual company and distraction locks.

💻 Companion Co-Working

Corti sits in a custom desk, typing alongside the user. Pause/Stop triggers alert dialogue. If challenge lockout is checkmarked, pausing requires immediate shaking device validation.

🌱 Garden — Habit Bloom & Shop
Check items grow plots. Spend Sparks to customize Corti.

🚿 Watering animations

Watering plot plants triggers watering can float details. Seeds grow to sprouts, then blooming flowers over days.

✨ Dopamine customizer shop
Accumulate sparks to unlock Headphones, Scarves, Hearts, and Crowns. Equipping items adds overlays to Corti across the entire app interface.
