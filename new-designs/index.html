Home Tab (Endel Clone)

Mascot-Driven Focus Dial

Our central orange button is inspired by Endel's generative flow. But dry soundscapes don't offer long-term hook loops. By positioning Corti directly inside the focus progress dial, we anchor the session's goal onto Corti's state.

The Yazio Calorie Tracker Principle
Just as Yazio uses a character to make counting numbers feel less clinical, Corti sits in the center of the dial to make ADHD focus sessions feel like checking on a companion rather than looking at a stopwatch.
Interactive Dial Feedback
When you click "Start Focus Session", the dial progress ticks down and Corti instantly puts on reading glasses and begins concentrating. This shifts the interaction from passive sound listening to active joint focus.
Sleep Tab (Somora / Sleepy)

Cozy Sleep Accompaniment

The sleep stages & sounds (inspired by Somora and Sleepy) are critical utilities, but they lack daily retention when the user is tired. By linking these states to Corti, we make wind-down times warm and friendly.

The Finch Virtual Pet Loop
When you enter sleep mode, Corti puts on a cozy sleeping cap and drifts to sleep. Sticking to your bedtime schedule lets Corti sleep peacefully, utilizing the virtual pet caretakers loop to prevent late-night screen scrolling.
Timer Tab (Pomodoro / Focus Kit)

Intense Focus Tasks

Pomodoro apps can feel rigid and frustrating for ADHD brains. Corti humanizes this clock, shifting attention from elapsed minutes to joint task accomplishment.

Visual Accountability Partner
As the clock ticks, Corti works alongside you. If you click pause, Corti looks up with curious dazed eyes, gently prompting you to resume without using standard nagging push alerts.
Habits Tab (HabitKit Clone)

Habit Kit Garden Growth

ADHD users struggle to maintain streaks on standard grid trackers (like HabitKit). We gamify this screen by transforming the tracking grid into a digital garden.

Finch Habit-Sprout Reward Mechanism
Clicking checkmark on "Hydration Boost" or "Morning Mindfulness" does not just fill a tile. It prompts Corti to fly down and water the tile, causing a green sprout to grow. This visual reward creates a powerful immediate hit of dopamine, driving high day-over-day engagement.
